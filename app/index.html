GEOSINT

Directions

Welcome to Geosint - a geolocation OSINT challenge platform.


If you're familiar with GeoGuessr, you already know how to play! Click on a challenge to begin.


To submit your guess, click a location on the mini-map and hit "Submit Guess". You'll receive immediate feedback - incorrect guesses will prompt you to try again, while correct guesses will reveal the flag!


Put your geospatial intelligence skills to the test!
